## Architectural Patterns Covered

These questions collectively test:

-   Distributed scheduling
-   High-throughput inference
-   GPU resource management
-   Caching and batching
-   Embedding indexing and ANN search
-   Data pipelines (offline + online)
-   Strong vs eventual consistency
-   Fault tolerance and idempotency
-   Real-time streaming systems
-   Distributed coordination
-   Scalability tradeoffs
-   Observability and reliability engineering
-   Geospatial indexing and routing
-   Concurrency control and race conditions
-   Inverted indices and ranking algorithms
-   Recommendation and personalization
-   Sandboxed execution environments
-   Lambda/Kappa stream architectures
